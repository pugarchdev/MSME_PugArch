# Audit Log Events

## Identity And Access

- User login and logout.
- Role, permission, and organization membership changes.
- Admin or master-admin approvals and rejects.

## Procurement

- Bid creation, submission, approval, publication, participation, document upload, clarification, evaluation, award, and cancellation.
- Purchase order creation, acknowledgement, delivery updates, invoice upload, GRN submission, and acceptance.

## Finance

- Payment initiation, webhook receipt, escrow hold, settlement release, refund, and reconciliation updates.

## Documents

- File upload, private document attachment, signed-url access, and document verification.

## Risk And Support

- Fraud alert review, compliance rule changes, dispute actions, grievance actions, and incident response actions.
