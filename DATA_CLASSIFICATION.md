# Data Classification

Public:
- Marketplace listing summaries, active banners, public buyer requirements, categories.

Internal:
- Procurement bids, ranking outputs, payment timelines, audit metadata.

Restricted:
- PAN, GSTIN, Aadhaar, bank account details, payment receipts, onboarding documents, private certificates.

Restricted data must not be logged raw, exposed through public URLs, or returned to unauthorized users.
